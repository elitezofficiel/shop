Care Instructions

Proper care will ensure your Premium Wireless Headphones maintain their performance and appearance for years to come.

Daily Care

  • • Wipe ear cushions with a dry, soft cloth after each use
  • • Store in the provided carrying case when not in use
  • • Avoid exposure to extreme temperatures
  • • Keep away from direct sunlight for extended periods

Battery Care

  • • Charge regularly, don't let battery completely drain
  • • Use only the provided USB-C cable for charging
  • • Avoid overcharging by unplugging when full
  • • Store with 50% charge if not using for extended periods

Cleaning

  • • Use slightly damp cloth for deeper cleaning
  • • Never submerge in water or use harsh chemicals
  • • Clean charging port with dry cotton swab
  • • Replace ear cushions when they show wear

What to Avoid

  • • Don't expose to moisture or liquids
  • • Avoid dropping or impact damage
  • • Don't use abrasive materials for cleaning
  • • Avoid extreme temperature changes
